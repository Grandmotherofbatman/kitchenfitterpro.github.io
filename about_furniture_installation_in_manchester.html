About Kitchen Fitter Pro

KitchenFitterPro, company called Innovation Carpentry LTD is a team of professionals who provide assembly, disassembly and repair services for furniture. Our experience of over 20 years in the doors, arcatraves, roofing and extensions guarantees you the quality and careful assembly of your furniture. We are team of specialists capable of maximizing the result.
